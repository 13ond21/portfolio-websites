Summary: World Clock Meet is privacy-first. Core features work offline. We do not sell personal data. Optional Google Play Billing and AdMob process limited data as described below.

1. Who we are

This policy applies to the Android app World Clock Meet published on Google Play by Lucky Tools (Northern Ireland).

2. Data we process

  • On-device content you enter (calculations, lists, reminders, settings) — stored in private app storage on your device. Not uploaded to our servers (we do not operate an account cloud for this app).
  • Google Play Billing — if you buy Premium, Google processes the purchase. We only receive entitlement status via Play Billing APIs.
  • Google AdMob (free tier) — advertising ID and standard ad-request data per Google’s policies, used to show ads. Premium users do not see ads.
  • Support email — if you contact us, we process the content of your message to reply.
  • Crash / diagnostics — none by default unless you enable a Play-linked report later.

3. Permissions

Only permissions required for the feature you use (for example notifications for reminders, or microphone for a tuner). Each is explained in-app or in the Play listing.

4. Children

Not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child provided data via support email, contact us and we will delete what we can identify.

5. Your rights & data deletion

You can clear app data or uninstall to remove local content. For a full data-deletion request (Play Console requirement), see: How to delete your data.

For privacy requests email autoaccentsni@gmail.com.

6. International transfers

Ads and Play may process data on Google infrastructure outside the UK/EEA under Google’s terms and safeguards.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the date above will change. Material changes will be reflected in the app store listing where required.
