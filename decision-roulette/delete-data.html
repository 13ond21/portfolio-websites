Choice Roulette has no accounts and no sign-in. Wheels and photos live only on your phone. We cannot delete that remotely. The only data the developer may hold is if you email support.

Delete everything on the phone

  1. Open Android Settings → Apps → Choice Roulette
  2. Tap Storage → Clear storage / Clear data, or
  3. Uninstall the app (removes local wheels, photos, and preferences)

Ads & purchases

Ad-related data is handled by Google AdMob under Google’s policies. Premium purchases are managed in Google Play (subscriptions / order history).

Email us to delete support messages

If you previously emailed support and want that correspondence deleted, write to autoaccentsni@gmail.com with subject Choice Roulette data deletion.
