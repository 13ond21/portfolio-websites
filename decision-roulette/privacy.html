Privacy Policy

App: Choice Roulette
Package: com.decision.roulette
Developer contact: autoaccentsni@gmail.com
Last updated: 18 July 2026

Choice Roulette is designed to keep your wheels and photos on your device. We do not require an account and we do not operate a cloud backup of your choices.

1. What the app stores on your device

  • Wheel titles, slice text, colours, weights, and settings
  • Optional photos you attach to slices (Premium) — stored only in app storage
  • Preferences (sound, theme, consent flags, Premium entitlement cache)
  • Spin history (when enabled / Premium features apply)

This data stays in the Android app sandbox. Uninstalling the app removes it.

2. What we (the developer) collect

We do not run our own analytics backend for Choice Roulette. If you email support, we receive whatever you send in that email (used only to reply).

3. Advertising (free tier)

The free version shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may process device advertising identifiers and similar data for ad serving, fraud prevention, and (where allowed) personalisation. We use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent where required.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob documentation.

Premium removes ads in-app after a successful Play purchase.

4. Purchases

Premium is sold through Google Play Billing (product premium_ad_removal). Google processes payment data under Google’s policies. We only receive purchase tokens / entitlement status needed to unlock Premium features.

5. Permissions

  • Photos / media — only when you choose an image for a wheel slice (Premium).
  • Internet — ads, consent, and Play billing.
  • Advertising ID — used by AdMob on the free tier where applicable.

6. Children

Choice Roulette is not directed at children under 13. Do not use the app to collect personal data from children.

7. Data deletion

Delete all local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in Android settings. See also Delete data.

8. Contact

Questions: autoaccentsni@gmail.com
