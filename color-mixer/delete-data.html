Delete my data

Hex-RGB Color Mixer stores preferences on your device only.

  1. Open Android Settings → Apps → Hex-RGB Color Mixer → Storage and tap Clear storage / Clear data.
  2. Or uninstall the app — that removes local app data for most devices.

If you need help, email autoaccentsni@gmail.com with the subject “Hex-RGB Color Mixer data deletion”.
