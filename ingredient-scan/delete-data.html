Delete my data

Ingredient Scan stores your profile, scan history, and caches on your device. We do not keep a cloud account with your dietary profile.

Clearing app storage or uninstalling removes local Ingredient Scan data on most Android devices.
  1. Open Android Settings → Apps → Ingredient Scan → Storage and tap Clear storage / Clear data.
  2. Or uninstall the app — that removes local app data for most devices.
  3. If you purchased Premium, manage or cancel the subscription in the Google Play app under payments & subscriptions (Google holds billing records, not us).

If you need help, email support.ingredientscan@gmail.com with the subject “Ingredient Scan data deletion”.
