Request data deletion

App: Fridge Share
Android package: com.luckytools.fridge_split
Developer contact: support.fridgesplit@gmail.com
Last updated: 18 July 2026

Use this page for Google Play’s data deletion request URL. Fridge Share has no accounts and no sign-in. Pantry data stays on your device. The only personal data the developer may hold is if you choose to email support.

What data exists where

  • On your phone (app storage) — pantry items, consumption logs, display name, and preferences. We cannot access this remotely.
  • With the developer — only support emails you send us (and our replies). There is no Fridge Share cloud account or pantry server.

Delete data on your device

  1. Open Fridge Share and delete individual items, or
  2. Android Settings → Apps → Fridge Share → Storage → Clear storage, or
  3. Uninstall the app (removes private app storage).

Request deletion of support email data

Email support.fridgesplit@gmail.com with subject “Fridge Share data deletion” and any details that help us find your thread. We will delete or anonymise support correspondence we hold, typically within 30 days.

Google Play

Purchase and install records are handled by Google under Google’s policies. We cannot delete Google account or Play history for you.
