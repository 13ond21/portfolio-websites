1. Who we are

Fridge Share is an Android app developed in Northern Ireland. This policy explains how the application handles information when you use it from Google Play.

2. What data the app handles

Depending on how you use the app, it may process:

  • Pantry items — names, quantities, units, communal/personal flags, owner labels.
  • Consumption logs — local history used for 7-day trend bars.
  • Profile display name — optional name used as the default label on personal items.
  • App preferences — onboarding completion and similar flags in private app storage.
  • Support messages — if you email us, we process the content of your message to reply.

3. Data that stays on your device

Pantry data is stored in private app storage on your phone (for example via SharedPreferences). We do not upload your pantry to our servers. Export/import uses a JSON file or share sheet that you initiate — we do not receive that export unless you choose to send it to us.

4. Network use & advertising

Core fridge features work offline. Network access is used for:

  • Google AdMob (free tier) — banner and occasional interstitial ads. AdMob may use your advertising ID and standard ad request data under Google’s advertising policies. You can limit ad personalisation in Android settings. Premium removes ads.
  • Optional links you open (privacy policy, website, support email).
  • Google Play Billing if you subscribe to Premium.
  • Normal OS / Play package updates.

We do not run a separate analytics SDK for behavioural profiling of your fridge contents.

5. Children

Fridge Share is a general lifestyle utility, not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Clear or edit pantry items inside the app at any time.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app storage.
  • Email us about support messages you previously sent.
  • Depending on your location (including UK GDPR), you may have rights to access, correction, erasure, and complaint to a supervisory authority.

7. Data deletion

See our data deletion page for Google Play’s required deletion URL. Local pantry data is removed when you clear app storage or uninstall. Support emails are deleted on request where we hold them.

8. Security

Data is kept in standard Android private storage. Protect the shared kitchen phone with a screen lock. No method of storage is perfectly secure.

9. Retention

Local data remains until you delete it or uninstall. Support email threads are kept only as long as needed to resolve your request, then deleted or minimised.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date above will change when we do. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
