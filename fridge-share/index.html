User guide

How it works

Fast to learn. No account wall.

1. Park one phone in the kitchen

Everyone opens Fridge Share on the shared handset and sees the same pantry.

2. Set your name once

Personal tab uses your name as the default label — like writing on your milk.

3. Add stock & tap Used

Log communal or personal items. Hit −1 Used when you take something.

4. Shop from the flat list

Low and empty items appear on the shop list so restocking is obvious.
