Delete my data

Interval Boxing Timer stores preferences on your device only.

  1. Open Android Settings → Apps → Interval Boxing Timer → Storage and tap Clear storage / Clear data.
  2. Or uninstall the app — that removes local app data for most devices.

If you need help, email autoaccentsni@gmail.com with the subject “Interval Boxing Timer data deletion”.
