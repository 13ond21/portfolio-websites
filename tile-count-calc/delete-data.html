Use this page for Google Play’s data deletion request requirement. This app does not create online user accounts. Most data lives only on your device.

How to delete your data

  1. Delete local app data immediately
    Android: Settings → Apps → Tile Count Calc → Storage → Clear storage / Clear data. Or uninstall the app to remove local storage for that install.
  2. Email a deletion request (optional but recommended for Play Console)
    Email autoaccentsni@gmail.com with subject “Tile Count Calc data deletion request”. Include your Google account email only if you previously contacted us — we do not have app logins. We aim to confirm within 30 days (usually much sooner).
  3. Ads (free tier)
    AdMob may process device identifiers under Google’s policies. Use Android advertising ID / privacy settings for those systems.
  4. Google Play purchases
    Payment records are retained by Google under Google’s policies. Premium entitlement is tied to your Play account.

What is deleted

  • On your device: inputs, lists, preferences, and other local content for that app install (via clear data / uninstall).
  • From us: support emails or other personal data we can identify from your request.

What may be kept

  • Limited email records for legal / abuse handling (typically up to 24 months).
  • Google Play payment records (held by Google).
  • Ad network data schedules (Google AdMob) for the free app.

No account system

Because there is no cloud account for Tile Count Calc, “account deletion” means removing local app data and any personal data we hold from support contact.
