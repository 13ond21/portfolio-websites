Request data deletion

App: Med Reminder
Android package: com.luckytools.med_reminder
Developer contact: autoaccentsni@gmail.com
Last updated: 17 July 2026

Med Reminder has no accounts and no sign-in. Medication lists and dose history live only on your phone. We cannot delete that remotely. The only data the developer may hold is if you email support.

What data exists where

  • On your phone — med names, times, adherence logs, settings, local Premium cache. We cannot access this remotely.
  • With the developer — only support emails you send (and our replies). No cloud med database.
  • Google / ads — Play Billing and AdMob process data under their policies. Use Google’s tools for those.

Steps to delete your data

  1. Delete local app data (immediate — no email needed)
    Android: Settings → Apps → Med Reminder → Storage → Clear storage / Clear data, or uninstall the app. This removes local medications and history. No confirmation from us is required because we never held that data on a server.
  2. In-app — Settings → Clear all app data (same effect for local lists).
  3. Support emails only
    Email autoaccentsni@gmail.com with subject Med Reminder data deletion request. Include the address you used to contact us. We delete identifiable support mail within 90 days and send one short confirmation reply. If you never emailed us, there is nothing for us to delete after you clear local storage.

Subscriptions

Cancel Premium in Google Play → Payments & subscriptions. That is managed by Google, not by clearing app storage.
