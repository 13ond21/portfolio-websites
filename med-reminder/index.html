Simple steps

How it works

Four calm steps. No sign-up.

1. Add a medicine

Name, optional dose, times of day, and which days. Stored only on this phone.

2. Open Today

See what is due. Tap Taken or Skip. The large clock keeps you oriented.

3. Turn on alerts if you want

Local notifications at dose times (you will be asked for permission on Android 13+).

4. Check your log

A personal record of what you marked. Free: 7 days. Premium: 30 days. Not clinical monitoring.
