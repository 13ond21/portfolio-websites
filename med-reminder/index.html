User guide

How it works

Five calm steps. No account. Everything stays on this phone.

1. Add a medication

Open Meds → Add. Enter a name, optional dose and notes, one or more times, and which days. Stored only on this device.

2. Use Today

See due doses under a large local time and date. Tap Taken or Skip. Undo if you tapped early.

3. Optional alerts

Local notifications at schedule times (Android 13+ permission). Exact alarms when allowed for reliability. Expand the notification for Taken / Skip / Snooze.

4. Adherence

Personal log of what you marked. Free: 7 days. Premium: 30 days. Share a short log for GP or family if you choose. Not clinical monitoring.

5. Free vs Premium

Free: up to 2 active meds + ads. Premium (~£2.99/mo): no ads, more meds, family profiles, pill photos, refill, backup. Cancel anytime in Play.

Not a medical device

Always follow your clinician’s guidance for real treatment. Med Reminder is a personal schedule tool — not professional medical advice.
