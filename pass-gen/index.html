User guide

How it works

One screen. Generate, jumble, copy. Premium custom words stay whole.

1. Pick Password or Passphrase

Password — random letters, numbers & symbols with clear toggles (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols, exclude look-alikes).
Passphrase — memorable word list with hyphen, underscore, space, or none.

2. Set length & options

Free: up to 32 characters / 5 words. Premium: up to 64 characters / 8 words. Strength meter shows estimated entropy as you generate.

3. Custom words (Premium)

Type full words separated by spaces — e.g. Fluffy 2019. Each word stays whole near the middle of the password (never letter-scrambled). Spaces only separate words; they are not added to the result.

4. Generate · Jumble · Copy

Generate creates a new value. Jumble moves your full custom word around as a block (letters stay together). Copy puts it on the clipboard. Nothing is uploaded.
