Request data deletion

App: Pass Gen
Android package: com.luckytools.pass_gen
Developer contact: autoaccentsni@gmail.com
Last updated: 17 July 2026

Use this page for Google Play’s data deletion request URL. Pass Gen is offline-first for password generation. Most data never leaves your phone.

Steps to delete your data

  1. Delete local app data (immediate)
    On Android:
    • Open Settings → Apps → Pass Gen → Storage
    • Tap Clear storage / Clear data
    Or uninstall Pass Gen. This removes local history, settings, and the cached Premium flag for that install.
  2. Clear history inside the app
    Open Pass Gen → History → clear, if you only want to wipe stored generations without full uninstall.
  3. Purchases
    Premium is managed by Google Play. Uninstall does not cancel or refund a purchase. To restore Premium after reinstall, use Restore purchases in the app with the same Google account.
  4. Support emails
    Email autoaccentsni@gmail.com with subject “Pass Gen data deletion request”. Include enough detail to find your message (approximate date / email address used). We will delete support correspondence we hold where applicable.
  5. Advertising partners
    Free-tier ads use Google AdMob (and mediation partners where configured). Reset or limit your advertising ID in Android settings. See Google’s ad settings for further controls.

What we do not store

We do not run a Pass Gen cloud vault. Generated passwords are not uploaded by the app. We cannot delete a password from “our servers” because it was never sent to us.
