1. Who we are

BMI Metrics is an Android app from a developer in Northern Ireland. This policy explains how the Android application handles information when you use it on Google Play.

2. What data the app handles

Depending on how you use the app, it may process:

  • Content you enter — calculations, lists, reminders, settings, notes, or other inputs for the feature you use.
  • App preferences — theme, units, notification toggles, and similar settings stored locally.
  • Purchase status — Google Play may confirm Premium ownership so the app can unlock paid features.
  • Advertising identifiers (free tier) — used by Google AdMob to show ads, under Google’s policies.
  • Support messages — if you email us, we process the content of your message to reply.

3. Data that stays on your device

Core BMI Metrics content is stored in private app storage on your phone or tablet. We do not operate a BMI Metrics cloud account or sync server for that content in the normal case. Uninstalling the app or clearing app storage removes local data for that install.

4. Purchases and Google Play

Optional Premium is sold through Google Play Billing. Payment details are processed by Google, not by a BMI Metrics payment server. We only receive entitlement status needed to unlock features. Manage subscriptions in Google Play → Payments & subscriptions.

5. Advertising

The free version may show ads via Google AdMob (and mediation partners where configured). Ad networks may process advertising ID and standard ad-request data. Premium removes ads. Use Android advertising ID / privacy settings and Google’s tools for ad choices.

6. Children

BMI Metrics is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data via support email, contact us and we will delete what we can identify.

7. Your choices and rights

You can clear app data or uninstall at any time. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold (for example support emails). Contact us at autoaccentsni@gmail.com.

8. Data deletion

For Google Play’s data deletion requirement, see our dedicated page: Request data deletion. Device deletion is immediate when you clear storage or uninstall. Email requests are normally handled within 30 days.

9. Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate to a local-first app. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Protect your device with a lock screen and keep the OS updated.

10. Retention

  • On device: until you clear data or uninstall.
  • Support email: typically up to 24 months, or longer if required for legal claims.
  • Play / ads: retained by Google under their policies.

11. International

Google Play and AdMob may process data on infrastructure outside the UK/EEA under Google’s terms and safeguards.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date above will change. Material changes will be reflected in the Play listing where required.
